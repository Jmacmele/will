About Us

Our Story: The Birth of GlowlightCompany

GlowlightCompany began its journey in 2019 in Nakuru as a purely photography-focused company. Founded by Willis Odhiambo, who is now our CEO, we set out with a vision to capture life's precious moments with precision and artistry. Over the years, our passion and commitment to excellence allowed us to grow and expand our services. Today, we offer a diverse range of creative and professional solutions to meet the evolving needs of our clients. Our mission remains the same: to provide unique and top-notch services, ensuring that every client experiences a different taste of excellence with GlowlightCompany.

Meet Our Skilled Team
